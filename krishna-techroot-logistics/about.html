Our Story

From Jaipur to Pan-India

Krishna Techroot Logistics was incorporated in September 2022 in Jaipur, Rajasthan — with a clear vision to become India's most preferred surface transport company. Starting from our operations hub at Diggi Road, Sanganer, we have grown to serve over 700 customers across the nation.

Our journey began in the textile sector — building a specialist corridor for grey cloth and fabric cargo from Tamil Nadu to Rajasthan. Today, we handle everything from 50 kg parcels to 150-tonne ODC consignments, serving both B2B and B2C clients with equal dedication.

With a team of 25 dedicated professionals and a sales-led approach that puts clients first, we have built lasting relationships across Rajasthan, Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, and Gujarat — and beyond.
