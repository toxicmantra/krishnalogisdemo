Our Services

Surface transportation solutions for every cargo type — from 50 kg parcels to 150-tonne ODC consignments, anywhere in India.

Service 01

Full Truck Load (FTL)

We provide the ideal choice for transporting substantial quantities of freight using trucks of any size. Our vast network is strategically positioned near raw material sources, plants, and customers throughout India — ensuring efficient and timely deliveries at competitive prices.

Whether you need a single truck or a dedicated fleet for recurring shipments, our FTL service guarantees safe handling, committed delivery timelines, and transparent communication throughout the journey.

Any Truck Size Pan India Competitive Rates Time-Bound
Service 02

Part Truck Load (PTL / Parcel)

The size of your shipment is inconsequential. With our extensive pan-India network and customer-centric hub consolidation, we ensure reliable, safe, and cost-effective movement of all your transportation requirements — starting from a minimum of 50 kg.

Our company hubs efficiently consolidate and expedite shipments, ensuring swift and seamless transportation whether your cargo is small or large. Perfect for B2B and B2C clients who need flexible, affordable logistics.

Min 50 kg Hub Consolidation B2B & B2C Cost-Effective
Service 03

ODC — Over Dimensional Cargo

With years of experience, professionalism, and specialised knowledge, we excel in transporting oversized and over-dimensional consignments across challenging terrains and long distances throughout India.

Our ODC capacity handles heavy goods from 100 to 150 tonnes — including industrial machinery, factory equipment, and structural components. Safe handling and route planning are our top priorities for every ODC shipment.

100–150 Tonne Heavy Machinery Route Planning Safe Handling
Service 04

Warehousing & Storage

Our modernised and latest-equipped hubs provide warehousing and storage facilities as part of our integrated logistics solution. The hub-and-spoke system ensures your cargo is stored safely and dispatched efficiently.

Combined with multi-location pickup and delivery, single-point billing, and key account management — we offer a complete logistics ecosystem, not just transport.

Hub & Spoke Storage Facility Modern Hubs Integrated Logistics
Service 05

Door-to-Door Delivery

Complete pickup-to-delivery service across India. Our sales team visits clients directly, understands requirements, and ensures seamless door-to-door logistics — whether you're a business shipping bulk orders or an individual with a 50 kg parcel.

With standardised billing, POD/e-POD proof of delivery, and round-the-clock services at major locations — we make logistics effortless for our 700+ customers.

Pickup to Delivery e-POD Sunday/Holiday Service Retail Distribution
Industry Specialist

Textile & Fabric Logistics

Our core expertise lies in textile logistics — particularly the grey cloth corridor from Tamil Nadu to Rajasthan. With deep industry knowledge, specialised material handling, and established relationships with 600–700 clients in the textile sector, we are the preferred logistics partner for fabric businesses across India.

Grey Cloth TN → Rajasthan Fabric & Garments Industry Expert
